Level 3 Interactable Objects:

Mandatory:
Ceiling Rocks(1 & 2): Fall from the ceiling, player must erase them to continue
Hole: Hole appears when Cat walks over it, falls in
Boxes: Must draw boxes to create a way out of the hole
Dingo & Box: Draw box over Dingo's head to knock him out

Optional:
Crystals: Slash with paintbrush to find fish
Cracked Rock: Erase to find fish
Weird Rock: Erase to find fish
Fish: There's a fish in the background, touch it to get it
